Hello there! As you may have read on my splash page, I am currently a third year Software Engineering and Education student at the University of California, Irvine. Professor Shindler's Birthday Graphic

Michael Shindler is an Associate Professor of Teaching at UCI who I happen to also be friends with and do research with. For his birthday, I collaborated with my friend Rose in order to make him a special graphic. Utilizing Canva, we developed a creative graphic that incorporated all of his favorite things, particularly crumple trees. The graphic features each of the tree nodes as a picture of his face. I learned how to maintain good image and text readability on busy backgrounds and the importance of composition.

BitHacks' Web Developer & Designer

BitHacks was UCI's first embedded systems hackathon. I had the opportunity to work as a web Developer and sole designer for the website. I worked closely with the Graphics and Web Development team to devise a thematic design, assets, and branding representative of the event’s mission. I utilized Figma to create the low, mid, and high-fidelity designs. I leveraged Next.js and TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and Framer Motion to creatively display significant event information, graphics, and integrate mobile responsiveness for a captivating user experience.

Soñando Sin Miedo's Web Developer & Designer

Soñando Sin Miedo is a student-led initiative aimed at providing low and no-income Latinx/Latine students with free academic support. I work as a web developer and designer for this website. I implemented interactive testimonies slider, contact page, and responsive design using Next.js and TypeScript, CSS, and Framer Motion. I designed wireframe and high-fidelity website prototypes in Figma, crafting creative components and website layout to reflect initiative’s goals. I also lead meetings amongst developers, streamlining progression of programming and designing.

CareTech

CareTech is a newly founded ICS club aimed at created equitable healthcare solutions through innovative technology. I am currently the Marketing Head of CareTech and have had the opportunity to work on amazing projects, such as designing their website and upgrading their logo. I designed the website solo using Figma. I honed my website designing skills by working on designing a website with multiple pages, but I plan to work on a redesign once I am no longer taking summer classes and working!

Everskies Designer

I worked as a game assets designer for the web game and app Everskies, a doll dress up game. I mainly created sets, which were a collection of clothing assets that were grouped together. I utilized Procreate and ibisPaintX on a 340x221 scale canvas in order to create designs. I collaborated and contributed to on-site events alongside other designers from around the world to formulate relevant, thematic designs. I developed a strong knowledge of color theory and strengthened by artistic skills as a self taught artist. Items I have created and uploaded to the site can be seen in the image.
