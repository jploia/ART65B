Hello there! As you may have read on my splash page, I am currently a third year Software Engineering and Education student at the University of California, Irvine. I have experimented a lot with UI/UX design, digital art, and creative projects in the past. I value the arts because of their capabilities of showcasing emotion.

Professor Shindler's Birthday Graphic

Michael Shindler is an Associate Professor of Teaching at UCI who I happen to also be friends with and do research with. For his birthday, I collaborated with my friend Rose in order to make him a special graphic. Utilizing Canva, we developed a creative graphic that incorporated all of his favorite things, particularly crumple trees. The graphic features each of the tree nodes as a picture of his face.

BitHacks' Web Developer & Designer

BitHacks was UCI's first embedded systems hackathon. I had the opportunity to work as a web Developer and sole designer for the website. I worked closely with the Graphics and Web Development team to devise a thematic design, assets, and branding representative of the event’s mission. I utilized Figma to create the low, mid, and high-fidelity designs. I leveraged Next.js and TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and Framer Motion to creatively display significant event information, graphics, and integrate mobile responsiveness for a captivating user experience.
